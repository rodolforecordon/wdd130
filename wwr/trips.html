Trips Details Equipment
Junior Perfect for families and first-timers! Float down the gentle currents of the scenic Sunstone Canyon. This trip features light Class I-II rapids, beautiful landscapes, and plenty of opportunities for swimming and wildlife spotting. It's a safe and relaxing introduction to the world of rafting.

Provided by us:

  • Standard Personal Flotation Device (PFD)
  • Helmet
  • Paddle

What you should bring:

  • Swimwear & Towel
  • Sunscreen
  • Water shoes or sandals with a heel strap
Mid Ready to up the excitement? Navigate the thrilling twists and turns of the Roaring Serpent River. This intermediate trip will test your paddling skills with sustained Class III rapids and faster currents. Teamwork is essential to conquer the "Serpent's Tail" section!

Provided by us:

  • Standard Personal Flotation Device (PFD)
  • Helmet
  • Paddle
  • Splash jacket & Wetsuit

What you should bring:

  • Secure, closed-toe footwear(river shoes or old sneakers)
  • Synthetic, non-cotton layers for warmth
Senior For experienced rafters only. This trip tackles the legendary Chaos Canyon, a demanding run with technical and powerful Class IV-V rapids, steep drops, and continuous white water. This is a physically demanding adventure that requires previous rafting experience and a mandatory pre-trip safety orientation.

Provided by us:

  • Type V Rescue PFD
  • Full-cut river helmet
  • High-performance paddle
  • Full wetsuit or drysuit (depending on season)

What you should bring:

  • Excellent physical condition
  • Sturdy, closed-toe river shoes or boots
